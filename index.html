About YouMatter

YouMatter is a mental health-focused company dedicated to supporting emotional well-being through innovative journaling tools. YouMatter was created through a spontaneous and meaningful brainstorming session at a library. While discussing possible names for our product, we initially coined the name "EuWrite," combining "Eugene" and "write" relating it to one of the founders' friend who is going through mental difficulties and the core act of journaling.

Over time, the name evolved naturally to "YouWrite," reflecting a more direct and personal connection to the user. This shift emphasized the importance of the individual's voice and story. From this, the company name "YouMatter" was born, capturing the central mission of our business, to affirm and support every person's mental health and emotional well-being.

The name "YouMatter" is a daily reminder to our users that their feelings, thoughts, and life experiences are important. It serves as a source of encouragement and mental health support, empowering individuals to prioritize their self-care and emotional resilience through guided journaling. YouMatter was founded for awareness and self-reflection, helping individuals initiate their mental wellness journey in a gentle, non-clinical way.
